Overview of my career so far and career aspirations.

After almost a decade of retail work (most of which spent as Assistant Store Manager level - the best level to be at) while trying to break into the media industry, I finally stumbled upon a YouTube Channel Coordinator role with Moonbug Entertainment, the owners of now the most successful kids TV show in the world, CoComelon.

During my time at Moonbug, I've been promoted twice, and now lead the strategy on some of our mid-level shows, Gecko's Garage and Oddbods. I line manage 5 junior colleagues and I also help out our Dev team in planning, testing and launching new tools and softwares.

Following on from that last point, I'm now looking at roles which can combine my skills learned so far - Excel, data analysis, project management, presenting, leading a team - and combining it with my newly learned skills in software.

My ideal roles would be either Data Analyst or a QA Tester. I'm a very good communicator, I love working in teams and I really like graphs and numbers. I'm also great fun at dinner parties, in case it wasn't obvious.
